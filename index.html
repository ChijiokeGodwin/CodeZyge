ABOUT

CodeZyge Academy runs a software development training subsidized by a community of philantropist, tech lovers, software engineers and developers. It aims to build and boast of great and responsible startups with innovative business models that can flourish over time.
A registration fee of N1,000 is required to show commitment and each session runs intensively for three months.
We are on the lookout for students passionate about technology, knowledge, able to work under pressure with entrepreneurial and problem solving mindsets. Accepted students will be taught IT skills with a focus on solving problems and creating businesses.
We partner with recognized firms, bootcamps and individuals across the IT sector and business world and periodically host seminars featuring our partners as guests.
